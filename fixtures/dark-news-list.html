Game News

Real-Time Royalty Arrives on macOS

This fixture represents a dark game news listing where the story container has a dark visual treatment, but the readable text color is set on nested copy elements.

Light Reader should detect this as dark reading content even though the candidate container itself does not expose a light text color directly.

After activation, the story copy should be readable on the selected light background and the nested dark panel should not hide the text.

A Second Story Keeps the Listing Article-Like

News pages often contain several long excerpts, release dates, sharing controls, product cards, and navigation links around the real prose.

The detector should tolerate that structure when there are still multiple substantial visible paragraphs in a central dark reading area.
